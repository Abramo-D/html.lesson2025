Young Ninja Ryu Hayabusa receives a letter informing him that his father Ken has left for a fight to the death, having not returned Ken has intrusted Ryu with their Clans secret Dragon Sword, he must take the Dragon Sword and venture to America. Seeking to Avenge his father and find Answers Ryu sets off. On his journey Ryu will learn of the Demon Statues, do battle with The Malice Four followers of the Almighty Evil JAQUIO, and become intertwined with the goals of the CIA. Ninja Gaiden features a dramatic storyline brought to life by TECMO’s Cinema Display, showing Movie-like scenes, with techniques such as Close-ups and different camera angles, with the addition of sound effects and music, and not only is the story told in such an engaging way, but the Gameplay features lightning-fast Ninja Action. As Ryu you’ll have acrobatic techniques, Ninja Magic attacks, and weaponry. All of which will be necessary to master across this challenging 20 stage adventure.
