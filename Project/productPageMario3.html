The long-anticipated Sequel is finally here. Bowser Along with his children The Koopalings have conquered each of the seven kingdoms, stolen their King’s magical wands, and transformed them into animals. It’s up to Mario and Luigi to thwart Bowsers plans, and take the fight to the Airships controlled by the Koopalings to take the Magic Wands back and restore the Kings to their rightful selves. There is a whole slew of new power ups and items to assist Mario and Luigi in their quest. The Super Leaf, and Tanooki Suit can allow the Brothers to fly though the air. The Frog Suit greatly increases underwater speed and agility. The Hammer can break though large rocks and clear a path though the overworld. The Music Box can put enemies to sleep and the brothers can slip past them. All this and more await in this thrilling 3rd instalment of the Super Mario Bros adventures.
